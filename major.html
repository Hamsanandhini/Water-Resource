Major multi purpose projects in India,Their location and uses

A multi purpose project is a large scale hydro project often including dams of water retention,canals for irrigation,water processing and pipelines to supply water to cities and power generation.

These often include transportation improvements and industrial growth.

They are also developed to reduce the dangers of flooding.

  • Thungabhadra Project of Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka
  • Hirakud dam
