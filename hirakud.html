Hirakud Dam


The main hirakud dam is in Sambalpur ,Orissa on the river mahanadi for about 15 KMs.​

It's the world's longest dam and its of 4801.2 metres long. ​

The project hirakud migrates irrigation of an area 11.98 Lakh hectares .

The presently installed power generation is of capacity 27.2MW.​

The construction of dam began in 1947 and was opened in 1957.

The height of the dam is 200 feet and it can hold 4,821,550 litres of water.

It is one of the first major multipurpose river valley projects started after India's independence.

Uses of Hirakud Dam
  • It Controls Flood
  • It generates hydropower power generation
  • It supplies Water for Irrigation purpose
