Thungabhadra Dam


The dam is in Hosapete,Vijaya Nagara,Kopal,Karnataka

It starts and flows through the state of Karnataka , before flowing along the border between Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and ultimately joining the Krishna River near Gundimalla village in Jogulamba Gadwal district of Telangana.

Thungabhadra dam also known as Pampa Sagar is a dam constructed over the thungabhadra river.

The construction of the dam began in 1949 and was opened in 1953

The capacity of the dam is 101 TMCFT

Its height is 162 feet and length is 2449 meters

Uses of Thungabhadra Dam
  • It serves the irrigation of the state
  • Controls the flood
  • Helps generate electricity
