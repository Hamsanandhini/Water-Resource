Importance of Water


The main 3 importance of water are for consumption of:

  1. Humans
  2. Plants
  3. Animals

HUMANS

  • Water regulates body temperature.
  • Water forms salaiva and mucus.
  • Water circulates oxygen throughout body.
  • Water helps in hydration.
  • Water helps maintaining BP.
  • Water makes minerals and nutrients accessible throughout body.
  • Water protects kidney damages
  • Helps in weight loss.

PLANTS

  • Water is essential germination of seeds.
  • Water is required for the growth of plant roots.
  • Water helps in convertion of starch to sugar in plants.
  • The plant requires water in hydraulic process.
  • Water is needed multiplication of soil organism and nutietion.
  • Water is used transipiration of nutrients from the soil to green plant issues.
  • Plant cells grow by increasing in volume for that it requires water

ANIMALS

  • Animals need fresh water for their bodies to function
  • Water is necessary for digestion and absorption of feed for animals
  • All chemical reactions in the bodies of animals use water as a medium.
  • Water helps break down food and keep organisms cool.
